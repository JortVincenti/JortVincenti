PLAY

Rules

  1. You place your colored stone next to a previously placed stone turn by turn
  2. Each time you place a stone on the board the stones of the other player will be changed to your color
  3. Try to end up with the most discs of your colour to win

Stats

  1. Total Number of Wins/Loses
  2. Total Number of Games
  3. Total time played
